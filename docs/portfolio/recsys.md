**Recommendation systems**, also known are a type of information filtering system that aims to predict the preferences or interests of users by analyzing data. They are widely used in various applications, such as e-commerce, streaming services, social media, and more. The primary goal of these systems is to provide personalized content or product recommendations to users based on their past behavior, preferences, and interactions.
